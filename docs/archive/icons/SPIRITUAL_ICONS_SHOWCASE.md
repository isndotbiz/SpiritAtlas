
## Visual Design Guidelines

### Spacing
- **Icon to text**: 8dp - 12dp
- **Icon to icon**: 8dp - 16dp
- **Icon padding**: 8dp - 16dp

### Alignment
- **In rows**: Use `Alignment.CenterVertically`
- **In columns**: Use `Alignment.CenterHorizontally`
- **In boxes**: Use `Alignment.Center`

### Contrast
- **Light backgrounds**: Use dark colors or add glow
- **Dark backgrounds**: Use light colors or vibrant hues
- **Colored backgrounds**: Ensure sufficient contrast ratio

### Glow Effects
- **Small icons (< 32dp)**: Consider disabling glow
- **Medium icons (32-64dp)**: Glow adds nice effect
- **Large icons (> 64dp)**: Glow enhances visual impact
- **Backgrounds**: Disable glow to reduce visual noise
