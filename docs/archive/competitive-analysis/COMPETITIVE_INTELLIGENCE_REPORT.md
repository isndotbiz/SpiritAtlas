## 10. RISK MITIGATION

### 10.1 Potential Pitfalls (Learn from Competitors)

**Risk 1: Aggressive Monetization Backlash**
- **Example:** The Pattern, Moonly lost trust by moving free features to paid
- **Mitigation:** Lock pricing tiers NOW. Never move features from free to paid.

**Risk 2: Negative Psychological Impact**
- **Example:** Co-Star's harsh tone causes anxiety, depression
- **Mitigation:** Use positive psychology framework. Test tone with mental health experts. Offer "Gentle Mode" settings.

**Risk 3: Platform Disparity**
- **Example:** Co-Star's Android version is "half-assed"
- **Mitigation:** Jetpack Compose ensures Android-first parity. Test both platforms equally.

**Risk 4: Late/Unreliable Updates**
- **Example:** Astrology Zone "always late" with monthly forecasts
- **Mitigation:** AI-generated content is instant. Set internal deadlines 2 days early.

**Risk 5: Privacy Breach/Data Selling**
- **Example:** User fear across all apps
- **Mitigation:** Transparent privacy policy. Local AI option (Ollama). Annual third-party security audit.

### 10.2 Quality Assurance Checklist

**Before Launch:**
- [ ] Test with 10+ beta users with anxiety/depression (tone check)
- [ ] Verify Android/iOS feature parity
- [ ] Legal review of privacy policy + terms
- [ ] Load testing (1000+ concurrent users)

- [ ] All accessibility standards met
