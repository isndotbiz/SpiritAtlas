## Research Summary

### Libra Constellation
- **Pattern**: Quadrangle shape representing balance scales
- **Key Stars**: Zubeneschamali (Beta - brightest, blue dwarf), Zubenelgenubi (Alpha - multiple star)
- **Meaning**: Arabic "southern claw" and "northern claw" (originally part of Scorpius)
- **Element**: Air sign
- **Ruling Planet**: Venus
- **Colors**: Pink, blue, balanced harmony
- **Mythology**: Only zodiac constellation named after inanimate object (scales)

**Sources:**
- [Space.com: Libra Constellation](https://www.space.com/21597-libra-constellation.html)
- [Constellation Guide: Libra](https://www.constellation-guide.com/constellation-list/libra-constellation/)

### Capricorn Constellation
- **Pattern**: Sea-goat shape (goat head/horns + fish tail)
- **Key Stars**: Deneb Algedi (Delta - brightest, mag 2.81), Dabih (Beta - yellow giant)
- **Meaning**: Latin "horned goat" or "goat horn"
- **Element**: Earth sign
- **Ruling Planet**: Saturn
- **Colors**: Dark green, grey, brown earth tones
- **Mythology**: Babylonian goat-fish SUHUR-MASH-HA, Greek Pan escaping Typhon, goat Amalthea who nursed Zeus

**Sources:**
- [Space.com: Capricornus](https://www.space.com/21414-capricornus-constellation.html)
- [Constellation Guide: Capricornus](https://www.constellation-guide.com/constellation-list/capricornus-constellation/)

### Gemini Constellation
- **Pattern**: Twin figures side-by-side, marked by Castor & Pollux stars
- **Key Stars**: Pollux (Beta - brightest, orange giant, mag 1.14), Castor (Alpha - sextuple system, blue-white)
- **Meaning**: Latin "the twins"
- **Element**: Air sign
- **Ruling Planet**: Mercury
- **Colors**: Yellow, silver, white for communication
- **Mythology**: Castor (mortal) and Pollux (immortal son of Zeus), united in heaven by Zeus

**Sources:**
- [Space.com: Gemini Constellation](https://www.space.com/16816-gemini-constellation.html)
- [Constellation Guide: Gemini](https://www.constellation-guide.com/constellation-list/gemini-constellation/)
