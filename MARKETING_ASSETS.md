**Accent Colors:**
- Chakra Red: #FF6B6B

- Throat Blue: #3B82F6
- Crown White: #F3F4F6

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif (System font or custom spiritual font)
- **Body:** Regular, sans-serif with excellent readability
- **Accent Text:** Semi-bold for emphasis
- **Line Height:** 1.5x for body text (readability)

### Imagery Guidelines
- **Style:** Cosmic, mystical, sacred geometry
- **Tone:** Modern minimal meets ancient wisdom
- **Color Treatment:** Purple/pink/gold gradients predominant
- **Quality:** All images 300+ DPI for App Store assets
- **Format:** PNG with transparency for overlays, JPG for backgrounds

### Icon Style
- **Sacred Geometry:** Use patterns like Flower of Life, Sri Yantra
- **Gradients:** Cosmic purple to pink or purple to gold
- **Glow Effects:** Subtle outer glow for depth
- **Minimalism:** Clean, recognizable at small sizes

**Q: Is SpiritAtlas really free?**
A: Yes! Core features including full spiritual profiles, basic compatibility analysis, and all calculations are completely free. Premium AI-powered personalization is available through optional subscription.

**Q: How is my data kept private?**
A: SpiritAtlas is designed privacy-first. All calculations happen on your device offline. We offer local AI (Ollama) that generates insights entirely on your device. Optional cloud AI requires explicit consent. We never sell your data, period.

**Q: What makes SpiritAtlas different from Co-Star or The Pattern?**
A: SpiritAtlas is the ONLY app combining 5 spiritual systems (Numerology, Astrology, Ayurveda, Human Design, Tantric) in one platform. Competitors focus primarily on astrology. We also offer privacy-first design with offline calculations - something no competitor provides.

**Q: Do I need internet to use SpiritAtlas?**
A: No! All core calculations work completely offline. Internet is only needed for optional cloud AI features. You can create profiles, generate birth charts, and analyze compatibility without any internet connection.

**Q: How accurate are the calculations?**
A: Extremely accurate. We use NASA-grade astronomical data for astrology, traditional formulas for numerology, and authentic Ayurvedic principles. Our app has 113/113 tests passing, ensuring calculation accuracy and reliability.

**Q: Can I use this for relationship compatibility?**
A: Absolutely! Compatibility analysis is one of our strongest features. Analyze relationships across 6 dimensions: Numerology, Astrology, Tantric, Energetic, Communication, and Emotional. Get instant detailed reports with strengths, challenges, and recommendations.

**Q: What are the 119 custom images?**

**Q: What is Human Design?**

**Q: Can I create profiles for other people?**

**Q: What's the difference between Chaldean and Pythagorean numerology?**
