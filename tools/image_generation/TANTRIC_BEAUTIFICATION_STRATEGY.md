## Sacred Union Symbolism Research

### Core Tantric Principles
1. **Divine Masculine (Shiva)**: Pure consciousness, witness awareness
   - Colors: Gold, white, solar
   - Symbols: Triangle pointing up, sun, lingam
   - Energy: Static, stable, witnessing

2. **Divine Feminine (Shakti)**: Creative power, dynamic energy
   - Colors: Purple, pink, red, lunar
   - Symbols: Triangle pointing down, moon, yoni
   - Energy: Dynamic, flowing, creating

3. **Sacred Union**: Balance of polarities, creation through union
   - Symbols: Vesica piscis, Sri Yantra, interlocked triangles
   - Result: Bliss, enlightenment, divine love
   - Visual: Two energies merging while maintaining individuality

### Sacred Geometry Elements
- **Sri Yantra**: 9 interlocking triangles (5 feminine, 4 masculine)
- **Vesica Piscis**: Two circles overlapping (cosmic womb)
- **Lotus**: Purity, transcendence, spiritual awakening
- **Infinity Symbol**: Eternal flow between polarities

### Color Psychology
- **Purple/Violet**: Crown chakra, spiritual connection, divine feminine lunar energy
- **Gold**: Solar plexus power, divine masculine solar energy, spiritual wealth
- **Pink**: Heart chakra, sacred love, divine compassion
- **White**: Pure consciousness, unity, enlightenment
- **Red**: Root/sacral chakra, life force, kundalini
